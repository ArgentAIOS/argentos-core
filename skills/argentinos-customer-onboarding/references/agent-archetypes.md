# Agent Archetype Library (Initial Set)

Use these as defaults, then customize per customer context.

## 1. IT Monitor

- Focus: Infrastructure and system health
- Triggers: Alerts, downtime signals, threshold breaches
- Outputs: Incident classification, summaries, escalation recommendations

## 2. Dispatch Coordinator

- Focus: Routing work to correct owner
- Triggers: New tickets/messages/tasks
- Outputs: Priority labels, ownership assignment, queue balancing

## 3. Compliance Guardian

- Focus: Policy and audit adherence
- Triggers: Sensitive actions, policy-bound workflows
- Outputs: Approval gates, compliance evidence, exception flags

## 4. Customer Communications

- Focus: External status and response consistency
- Triggers: Customer-facing incidents and updates
- Outputs: Draft replies, update cadences, communication logs

## 5. Executive Intelligence

- Focus: Leadership visibility and decisions
- Triggers: Daily/weekly reporting windows, incident milestones
- Outputs: Executive briefings, risk summaries, trend insights

## 6. Vendor Monitor

- Focus: Third-party dependencies and renewals
- Triggers: Vendor alerts, SLA breaches, renewal windows
- Outputs: Risk flags, follow-up tasks, vendor communication drafts

## 7. Orchestrator

- Focus: Cross-agent coordination
- Triggers: Multi-step workflows spanning teams
- Outputs: Handoff control, workflow state, rollback or escalation paths
