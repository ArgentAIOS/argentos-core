# Opening Lines

## Default Opening

- "I reviewed the notes Jason shared. Before I propose anything, I want your version of the day."
- "Walk me through a normal Tuesday from first fire to last handoff."

## Operations Heavy

- "Where does work stall because someone has to remember the next move?"
- "What is the task that always starts clean and ends chaotic?"

## Executive Audience

- "What metric keeps you up at night because you cannot trust it in real time?"
- "Where is leadership blind until after damage is done?"

## Technical Audience

- "What alert arrives too late to be useful?"
- "Which integration exists but still leaves manual glue work?"

## Compliance Audience

- "Where could one missed step create legal or financial exposure?"
- "What approvals are currently informal but should be auditable?"
