# Transition Lines

## Discovery -> Proposal

- "I heard three recurring failure points. I will map each to a dedicated agent role."
- "Let me shift from diagnosis to design and show the operating model."

## Proposal -> Live Build

- "If this roster matches your reality, I will generate the deployment artifacts now."
- "I am moving from concept to concrete documents you can execute."

## Live Build -> Close

- "You now have the initial architecture and execution documents."
- "Before we close, I need one phase-1 anchor from you."

## If Customer Pushes Back

- "Good correction. I will adjust the scope to match your operating constraints."
- "I am optimizing for what you can deploy, not what sounds impressive."

## If Time Is Running Short

- "I will finish the minimum deployable set now and queue secondary items as phase two."
- "Let us lock day-one impact first, then expand."
