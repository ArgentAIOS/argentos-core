# Workflows

Workflows let you build automations visually. Drag blocks onto a canvas, connect them, and your AI agents do the work.

**No code required.** If you can draw a flowchart, you can build a workflow.

## What Can You Build?

- **Content pipeline** -- Your AI writes a blog post every Monday, another AI reviews it for SEO, a third creates social media posts, and everything gets published automatically
- **Lead nurture** -- When someone signs up, AI researches their company, writes a personalized welcome email, then follows up on a schedule
- **Client onboarding** -- New client form triggers AI to create their account, assign a team member, schedule a kickoff call, and send a welcome packet
- **Daily briefing** -- Every morning, AI scans the news in your industry, summarizes what matters, and sends it to your inbox

## Quick Start

1. Go to **Operations > Workflows**
2. Click **+ New** in the sidebar
3. Drag a **Trigger** onto the canvas (this is what starts your automation)
4. Drag an **Agent Step** (this is your AI doing work)
5. Drag an **Output** (this is where the result goes)
6. Connect them by dragging from one dot to another
7. Click on any block to configure it in the right panel
8. Hit **Run** to test it

That's it. You just built your first AI automation.
